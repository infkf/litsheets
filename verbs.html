How Lithuanian Verbs Work

Almost all verb forms are derived from three principal parts (stems): the **Infinitive stem** (e.g., dirb-), the **Present Tense 3rd person stem** (e.g., dirb-a), and the **Past Tense 3rd person stem** (e.g., dirb-o). Verbs are grouped into 3 conjugations based on the vowel in the present tense 3rd person ending.
